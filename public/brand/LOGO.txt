Brand logo (header + footer)
============================

1. Save your image in THIS folder (public/brand/) as:

   logo.png

   You can use PNG, WebP, or SVG. If you use another filename or format,
   set in .env.local:

   NEXT_PUBLIC_BRAND_LOGO_SRC=/brand/your-file.webp

2. Recommended: square artwork, at least about 120×120 px (displays at 36px
   in the header and 56px in the footer).

3. After replacing the file, hard-refresh the browser if you still see the
   old image (browser cache).
